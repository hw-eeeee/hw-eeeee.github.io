About Me


Helloooo my name is Hui, currently a 3rd year student studying Computer Science at UNSW! I come from Singapore and Shanghai and I’ve spent the past 11 years living in the beautiful Sydney, Australia.

I have a strong passion for education, technology, and the arts. I believe that these interests have endless possibilities yet to be uncovered and would love to spend my life learning and exploring them!

When I have spare time, I love:
  • Playing the cello
  • Checking out new tech products
  • Going on walks
  • Exploring and learning about different cultures

I currently work as a cello teacher, guiding students of all ages through their personal musical journeys. Contact me if you’re interested to learn!

In university, I am involved in many societies which has greatly shaped and improved my university life. At CSESoc, I enjoy getting the hands-on experience in producing high impact events such as programming contests, hackathons, CTFs and many more meaningful competitions for UNSW computer science students. I also explored my creative side at CompClub where I design promotional materials including banners and create cute merchandise such as lanyards and keychains for workshop attendees.

Currently interested in the low-level side of computing and would love to learn more about Operating Systems. Also very interested in design and learning front-end programming!
